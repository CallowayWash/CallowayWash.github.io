About Me

I have been creating art since I could pick up a pencil and have loved every second of it. I tend to seek out new forms of art to try or test out, always willing to learn and expand my artistic abilities. Luckily, my school district, my vocational program, and my parents have all helped to facilitate this goal. There is much I could discuss, but here are key points worth mentioning should you be interested in hiring me.

What Qualifies Me?

  • 3+ years exerience with Adobe Products
  • 2+ years experience with vector graphics and 5+ years experience with raster graphics
  • Many years of experience with traditional mediums, such as graphite, charcoal, watercolor, acrylic, alcohol marker, and ink pens
  • Experience with jewelry making, wood working, print making, and calligraphy

What Have I Achieved?

  • Digital Media I, Digital Media II, and Web Development I Career Skills certificates
  • Pennsylvania Skills Certificate in Commerical and Advertising Art from the Pennsylvania Department of Education
  • Winning several competitions for my work, such as through SkillsUSA, Pittsburgh AdFlux23, and more
  • Creating logos and designs that have gone on to be sold as merchandise for small companies and organizations in my general area
